Allegorical Patterns
Intro
The Allegorical patterns refer to decorative patterns with a certain meaning. Since ancient times, the decorative patterns of ethnic minorities in China have often contained certain meanings. It often use figures, animals, flowers and birds, utensils and other traditional Chinese patterns, such as some auspicious characters, and use folk proverbs, auspicious words and mythological stories as themes, and use metaphors, puns, symbols, and homophonic expressions.

It changes with the emergence, migration, and social change of human beings， has a unique form, content, cultural carrying capacity and inheritance. Some are a symbol of good luck, and some are prosperous, and most of them are rich in meaning. Reflect people's pursuit and yearning for a better life.

For example, the allegorical patterns of ethnic minorities are usually displayed in structures, headdresses, religious paintings, fabric decorations and iconic patterns.
